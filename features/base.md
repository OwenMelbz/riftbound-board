the base is just an area for non-rune, non-legend cards to sit.

cards here can be:

- exhausted
- trashed
- recycled

You can right click on it to pick any of the above options to complete the action.

You can hover it and push E to exhaust
You can drag it onto the trash to trash it
You can hover it and push R to recycle it, a prompt should ask for confirmation.
