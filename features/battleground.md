There's 2 parts to battlegrounds, the card itself, then the battleground area where conflicts happen.

The card itself:
A battleground is a card which has a passive ability and is rotated 90*

If your deck contains multiple battleground it should stack all the battle grounds into the battle ground pile.

You can right click on the pile of battlegrounds and click "Next Battleground" to go to the next, you can cycle through them as many times as you like, or you can hover and over it and push S to switch to the next card, it should loop.

The battleground area:
There is an additional zone that other cards can be played into, which should auto resize and fit the cards to the area

cards here can be:

- exhausted
- trashed
- recycled

You can right click on it to pick any of the above options to complete the action.

You can hover it and push E to exhaust
You can drag it onto the trash to trash it
You can hover it and push R to recycle it, a prompt should ask for confirmation.
